Places I Have Been and Would Like to Go

Travel Theme: Beach Vacations

While I enjoy travel accross the globe, one of my favorite types of travel are relaxing beach vacations. This page explores some of my most recent travel and some of my dream beach vacations. I am someone who enjoys 85 degree weather and constant sun, even the humidity!

My Dream Travel Destination: Maldives

A chain of islands southwest of India and Sri Lanka, the Maldives seems like the perfect destination. Warm and with water as clear as the eye can see. On my next trip to India, I will definitely make a stop to the Maldives.

My Winter 2015 Vacation: Cabo

A great week starting from the day after Christmas until New Years in Cabo. Although a warm destination, I was surprised that the weather never made it above low 70s. Still a great time with a large group of family and friends. I would definitely go back, but maybe during a warmer month.

Recommended Excursion:

  • Glass Bottom Boat Tours

My Summer 2013 Vacation: Turks and Caicos

One of my favorite trips, a relaxing beach vacation with my husband to celebrate his 36th birthday and our two year wedding anniversary.

Favorite Hotel:

  • Gansevoort Turks and Caicos
